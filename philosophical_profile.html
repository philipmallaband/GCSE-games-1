🧭 What's Your Philosophical Profile?

8 questions · No right answers · Find out where you really stand

Should belief in God be based on rational argument, or is it a matter of personal faith?

Philosophers have disagreed sharply on this. Some think careful argument can prove God exists. Others — like Kierkegaard — say faith is precisely about going beyond what reason can demonstrate. Others think the arguments fail and God is unlikely. And some sit genuinely in the middle.

Answer honestly — there are no correct answers. At the end you'll find out which philosophical tradition you naturally belong to.
